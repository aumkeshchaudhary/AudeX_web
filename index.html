About AudeX

AudeX combines advanced OCR and TTS technology to provide an accessible solution for converting text into speech and making images readable.

Concept: AudeX is built upon state-of-the-art Optical Character Recognition (OCR) algorithms that extract text from images with unmatched accuracy. These extracted texts are then processed through advanced Text-to-Speech (TTS) systems to create human-like voices for accessibility and usability.

Methodology: The OCR engine utilizes machine learning models trained on diverse datasets to ensure precision in text extraction across various languages and font types. Meanwhile, the TTS system is powered by neural network-based voice synthesis technology, ensuring natural intonation and clarity.

  • Extract text from images with precision
  • Listen to text in natural-sounding voices
  • Accessible and easy-to-use interface
  • Customizable voice settings for user preferences
  • Support for multiple languages and accents

How to Use AudeX

1. Upload an image containing text.
2. Click on the "Process" button.
3. Listen to the text synthesized into a natural voice.
